📚 Educational Content

📊 RSI Indicator

Relative Strength Index measures momentum. RSI <30 = oversold (buy), RSI >70 = overbought (sell).

📈 Support & Resistance

Support is a price floor where buying pressure emerges. Resistance is a ceiling where selling pressure increases.

🎭 Fear & Greed

Extreme fear (0-25) often signals buying opportunities. Extreme greed (75-100) often precedes corrections.

📉 Risk:Reward Ratio

Always aim for 2:1 or higher. Risk $100 to make $200+. This makes you profitable even with 40% win rate.

🕐 Time Frames

Use higher time frames (4H, Daily) for direction, lower (1H, 15m) for entry timing.

💰 Position Sizing

Never risk more than 2% of account on single trade. Protect capital above all.
